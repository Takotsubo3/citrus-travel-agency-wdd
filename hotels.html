Ibis Paris Bastille Opera 11th

Situated in the lively Bastille district, Ibis Paris Bastille Opera 11th offers convenient access to the Accor Arena, a key venue for basketball, gymnastics, and wrestling during the Paris 2024 Olympics. The hotel is just a short metro ride away from the arena, making it a prime location for Olympic attendees.

Renaissance Paris La Defense Hotel

Located in the modern La Défense business district, Renaissance Paris La Defense Hotel is just a stone's throw from the Arena La Défense, where key Olympic events like basketball and gymnastics will take place. The hotel offers direct access to public transportation, making it easy to reach various Olympic venues across the city.

Hotel Molitor Paris - MGallery

Hotel Molitor Paris - MGallery is ideally located near Roland-Garros Stadium, the iconic venue for tennis events during the Paris 2024 Olympics. It’s also close to Parc des Princes, where football matches will take place. This prime location offers guests easy access to some of the most exciting Olympic events.
